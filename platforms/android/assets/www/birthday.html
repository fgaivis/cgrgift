Make your child’s next birthday or team party one to remember with Pools and Play!

5,000-square-foot indoor playground facility offers five full play sets with rock-climbing walls, swings, slides, jungle gyms, and more to keep your child and their guests entertained for hours!

Rates:

  • Up to 25 kids: $255 Weekdays ($8.99 each additional child; $299 Friday, Saturday and Sunday
  • 15 kids: $220 Weekdays; $269 Friday, Saturday and Sunday
  • 10 kids: $175 Weekdays; $199 Friday, Saturday and Sunday

Private party room available. Playroom reservations consist of 90 minute time slots plus an extra 30 minutes prior to your party allotted for set up.
